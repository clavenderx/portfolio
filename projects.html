How I Work

My Design Process

01

Research & Discovery

Understanding user needs through interviews, surveys, and competitive analysis to uncover insights that drive design decisions.

02

Ideation & Wireframing

Sketching solutions and creating low-fidelity wireframes to explore different approaches and validate concepts quickly.

03

Design & Prototype

Crafting high-fidelity designs with attention to detail, then building interactive prototypes for user testing.

04

Test & Iterate

Conducting usability tests, gathering feedback, and continuously refining the design based on real user insights.
